## First person

[Cognate set 1abs](CognatesetTable?#cldf:1abs)

* reconstructible: [Form pc-one](FormTable?no_translation#cldf:pc-one)
* found throughout the family:
    * grammaticalization of emphatic marker **rə*
    * coalescence of *wɨ* to *u*
* appearance of *j* in [Language pem](LanguageTable?#cldf:pem) and [Language pan](LanguageTable?#cldf:pan) -- via /əwɨ/ > /ɨwɨ/ > /ɨu/ > /ju/? ([meira2002first](sources.bib?with_internal_ref_link&ref#cldf:meira2002first): 259)
* additional *hɨ* in Manapiarean
